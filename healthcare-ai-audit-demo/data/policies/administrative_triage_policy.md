# Northstar CareOps Administrative Triage Policy

This synthetic policy exists only for the Ryva healthcare demo.

1. The patient intake triage agent may summarize inbound requests for operations staff.
2. The agent may recommend administrative routing only.
3. The agent must not diagnose, recommend treatment, or make final patient-care decisions.
4. Any urgent symptom language, self-harm language, breathing difficulty, chest pain, or similar red-flag content requires human review.
5. PHI-like contact details must be masked in summaries and governance artifacts where possible.
6. Billing and scheduling requests may route without clinical decision-making when no urgent content appears.
